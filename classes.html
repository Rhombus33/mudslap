Private group classes

Please contact us to schedule a private group class. Email mudslapceramics@gmail.com with group size and preferred date.

Handbuilding class (5-15 participants)

Choose and complete a handbuilding project with clay, while learning some fundamental handbuilding techniques along the way. Perfect for beginners and those looking to explore their creativity. Choose a project for your group from bowl, mug, vase, palette or cat figurine.

Price per person: $50 for a 2.5 hour session.

Mini wheelthrowing class (5-10 participants)

Discover the art of mini wheelthrowing! In this class, you'll learn how to throw small pottery pieces on the wheel, such as mini bowls, tiny vases, and petite mugs. Perfect for those who want to try wheelthrowing in a fun and relaxed setting. Make, paint and take home up to 5 pieces each.

Price per person: $70 for a 2.5 hour session.

Wheelthrowing class (2 participants)

Learn how to throw pottery on the wheel in a small group setting with personalized instruction. This is a great way to try out wheelthrowing before committing to a series of classes. Take home up to 3 pieces each.

Price per person: $90 for a 2.5 hour session.
