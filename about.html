About

Mudslap ceramics is run by me, Diamond Wheeler! I'm a local bay area ceramic artist living in the Peninsula. I took my first pottery class in high school and later found myself teaching for the first time on a campus studio in college. Since then I've taught at various studios around the bay area and host private classes. I've been working with clay for over 10 years and have experience teaching classes for adults at all skill levels. I love to share my love of ceramics with other and help each student discover their own creativity through clay.
